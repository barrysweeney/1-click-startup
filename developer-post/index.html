Developer Guide

Product Vision

The end product is a system targeted at a business owner which will provide them and their employees with an interface to perform their business functions.

Employees of the business can use the product to fill in a series of digital forms which log important information about the processes they complete in their work.

The manager can use the product to complete managerial specific forms relating to accounting and stock orders.

This data will be piped through a process mining application and the results will be forwarded to the manager's dashboard.

This dashboard, exclusive to the manager, will allow them to track key productivity metrics and highlight employee engagement, as well as visualize the business processes to identify bottlenecks.

In this manner, we are digitising the bureaucracy of an organization.

Project Vision

The end project will be the technical implication of the product (as described in the vision) using tools such as Docker, shell scripts, and a Flask REST API webserver.

In addition to this, we also want to provide a testing framework with simulated users and managers using a tool such as PySelenium.

User Experience (UX) Design

We want to solve problems our user is facing and improve their overall work-flow. In order to do that we need to define our user and figure out more specific details regarding their current work-flows, including the services and platforms they use. We'll also take a look at the approaches being employed by others with similarities to our product.
